Nothing New Under the Sun

2011-02-02
Everything is a Remix, Part II is out today, and for me it's not a moment too soon.  As I wade further into this project of shameless solipsism and word-wasting - which will likely be almost entirely derivative of other people's thoughts and observations - it's good to be reminded that there's no such thing on earth as "pure originality."  If I can add just a little to each thing I steal, then maybe it's worth it.  How little counts as "just a little"?  We'll see.
