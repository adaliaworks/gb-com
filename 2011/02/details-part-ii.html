Details, Part II

2011-02-09
I guess this is the answer to yesterday's question:

Stanley Kubrick.  (Or, see the cached version here.)  The video is worth the two minutes.

It's a remarkable insight into the mind and manners of a guy who somehow managed to produce some of history's greatest films.  If he cares that much about boxes, can you imagine how much he sweated the composition of every frame he shot?  This level of attention to detail would cripple lesser men, but to him it was apparently a rather amusing and ordinary feature of his mental landscape.

And, you have to admit - the man sure could design a cardboard box!
