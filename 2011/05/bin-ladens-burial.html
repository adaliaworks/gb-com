Bin Laden's Burial

2011-05-11
I don't have anything to add to the Bin Laden death discussion, and it's old news anyway, but this article from today's Daily Mail struck me as particularly rich:
Osama Bin Laden's son has denounced the Al Qaeda leader's killing as 'criminal' and said he reserves the right to take legal action against America.
...He also branded his father's burial at sea as a 'humiliation' for his family.
Well, it's nice to know he's proud enough to still be capable of feeling "humiliation." For a descendant of the man who dubbed us the Great Satan, that guy sure does seem assimilated to Western Culture. I mean, no one really fears "Terrorism" these days, but the threat of a lawsuit hits every American pretty close to the bone. An anxious nation awaits his next move.
