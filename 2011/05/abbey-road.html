Abbey Road

2011-05-23
There's a webcam pointed at the world's most famous crosswalk.

Have college kids made a drinking game out of watching this during relatively low-traffic times and doing a shot whenever someone poses for their own version of the famous photo?  I assume so.

Also, I love watching people step out in front of fast-approaching cars and even busses, knowing with certainty that the vehicle will stop for them.  I've never seen the "stop for pedestrians at crosswalks" law so well observed.  Then again, I am relatively un-travelled.
